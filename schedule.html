Draft Schedule for ACT & MFPS

Talks will take place in five lecture rooms across three nearby buildings (see [venue map](venue-map.html)): Lecture Theatres A and B of the Computer Science Department Wolfson Building (use OERC Keble Road entrance); the Lindemann Lecture Theatre of the Clarendon Building (Parks Road), and the Dennis Sciama Lecture Theatre of the Dennis Wilkinson Building (Keble Road). Click the talks to find out where they are; they are also colour-coded.

The Southern and Midlands Logic and Semantics Seminar will also take place on Tuesday afternoon in Lecture Theatre B. The schedule for that is here). Speakers include Daniela Petrisan, Keji Neri, Zev Shirazi, Katya Piotrovskaya, Sam Speight, and Andrzej Murawski. ACT & MFPS participants are welcome to pop in to those talks (if there is space — and please don't eat their food unless you are in touch with Bartek!)
