The 7th International Conference on Applied Category Theory (ACT) and 40th International Conference on Mathematical Foundations of Programming Semantics (MFPS) will take place together at the University of Oxford, UK. ACT will take place 17 – 21 June 2024, and MFPS will take place 19 – 21 June 2024. The conferences will be preceded by the Adjoint School 2024.
