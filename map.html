Map of ACT & MFPS area

Talks will take place in Lecture Theatres A and B of the Computer Science Department Wolfson Building (Keble Road) and the Lindemann Lecture Theatre of the Clarendon Building (Parks Road).

There are plenty of food options on Little Clarendon Street, and Gloucester Green Food Market is not far away. Wellington Square and University Parks are handy picnic locations.

Please see other sites (e.g. Google maps, tripadvisor) for eat-in lunch/dinner venues, or chat to local people.
